Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as minor office works? ▼
Minor office works are small construction or improvement packages that fall short of a full refurbishment — things like redecorating a floor, replacing carpet tiles, adding a partition, upgrading lighting, or installing additional data points. These are scoped, contracted works with a defined programme, not ad hoc odd jobs.
How do you minimise disruption to our office? ▼
We agree a detailed programme with you before starting on site. Works are typically delivered in phases — by floor, zone or department — so teams are never displaced all at once. Where the scope requires it, we can discuss access arrangements that suit your building and occupancy.
Do I need a main contractor for small works? ▼
For anything beyond basic maintenance, yes. A contractor brings proper RAMS, site management, trade sequencing and a single accountable point of contact — reducing risk for you and ensuring the work meets building regulations and health & safety requirements.
Can you handle works across multiple offices? ▼
Absolutely. We regularly roll out programmes of minor works across multiple office locations for the same client, delivering a consistent standard and a single contract to manage.
What areas do you cover? ▼
We cover London, Kent, Surrey and Essex. If you are just outside these areas, get in touch — we consider projects on a case-by-case basis.
