If you're wondering, "How much does an office refurbishment cost in the UK?" you're not alone. Many businesses considering an office fit-out or renovation face this exact question. In this guide, we break down the typical pricing benchmarks, explain what influences costs, and help you understand what you might expect to pay in 2025. Whether you're planning a simple refresh or a complete office makeover, knowing the key factors can help you budget smarter and get the most from your workspace.

Office refurbishments vary, but a typical medium office is around 300–500 m² (3,000–5,000 sq ft). This could house 30–60 people, depending on the office layout. Costs scale with the size of the office space — larger spaces require more materials and labour. Different types of office refurbishment might involve cosmetic upgrades, new office design layouts, or smart office systems to improve functionality and productivity.

Refurbishment Cost in the UK: Per Square Foot in 2025

  • Basic office refurbishments: £40–£60 per sq ft
  • Mid-spec refurbishment: £60–£80 per sq ft
  • High-spec office refurbishment: £80–£100+ per sq ft (some projects reach £200)

For a 5,000 sq ft office:

  • Basic: £200,000–£300,000
  • Mid-range: £300,000–£400,000
  • High-spec: £400,000+

Factors Influencing Office Refurbishment Cost

  • Location: Offices in London and the South East carry higher labour and logistics costs. A central London project might cost 15–20% more.
  • Scope and type of refurbishment: Light redecoration costs less than layout changes or installing new systems.
  • Office furniture and IT: Budget £12–£32 per square foot. Cabling alone can cost £40–£60 per data point. Furniture costs can vary widely depending on specification.
  • Partitions and ceilings: Glass partitions may cost £150–£250 per m²; solid stud walls cost less. Ceiling upgrades also affect pricing.
  • Lighting and finishes: LED upgrades are typically £1.50 per square foot; premium lighting systems will cost more.

Office Fit Out or Refurbishment: What's Right for You?

A fit out usually refers to preparing a new office space for use. A refurbishment upgrades an existing office environment. Office fit outs often include core services and finishes, while refurbishments may retain more of the current infrastructure. Your decision may depend on whether you're moving into new office space or revitalising your existing office setup.

Office Refurbishment Costs: Average Cost Across the UK

Across the UK, average office fit-out costs in 2025 range from £70 to £110 per square foot. Businesses in the South East should allow for higher figures. Most companies budgeting around £80–£90 per square foot achieve a professional office setup that balances quality and value.

Cost Control Tips to Refurbish Your Office Efficiently

  • Painting a 5,000 sq ft office: ~£15/m² = £7,000–£8,000
  • Replacing carpets: ~£30/m² = £15,000
  • LED lighting: ~£7,500
  • Furniture (mid-spec): £60,000–£90,000
  • Structured IT cabling: ~£6,000

These typical figures reflect the scope of refurbishment and expected investment per component. Keep in mind that additional costs may apply depending on the complexity of the project.

Office Refurbishment Costs in London vs the UK

London refurbishment costs are higher — mid-spec office refurbs can hit £207 per square foot, compared to £180 in Manchester. South East projects often sit in between. Factors like labour costs, site access, and contractor demand impact the final price of the work. Workspace constraints and timing can also affect costs for an office refurbishment.
