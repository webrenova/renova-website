Project Overview

A complete exterior transformation

Lancaster House is a prominent commercial property on the high street in St Ives, Cambridgeshire. The building required a complete exterior refresh — the existing paintwork had deteriorated and the colour scheme no longer complemented its surroundings.

Renova Projects UK was appointed to deliver the full scope of exterior redecoration works, including erection of full scaffold, thorough surface preparation across all elevations, and application of a bold new colour scheme selected to better suit the building's setting and give it a clean, modern presence on the street.

All timber windows and entrance doors received a microporous coating — allowing the timber to breathe while providing long-lasting weather protection. Window frames, fascia, soffits and external panels were painted to a high standard throughout.

Scope of Works
  • 01
    Full scaffold erection Safe access to all elevations throughout the duration of the project
  • 02
    Zero emission welfare unit on site Our own self-contained welfare unit meant no disturbance to the building's occupying offices throughout the works
  • 03
    Surface preparation across all elevations Scraping, filling and putty repairs to windows and surrounding masonry
  • 04
    Painting of window frames, fascia, soffits & panels New colour scheme applied to all external joinery and panel elements
  • 05
    Microporous coating to all timber windows & entrance doors Breathable, weatherproof finish that protects and preserves the timber long-term
  • 06
    Scaffold strike and site clearance Full removal on completion, site left clean and clear
