Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a commercial office refurbishment take? ▼
Timelines vary based on project size and complexity. A straightforward office interior refurbishment might take just a few weeks, while larger commercial office refurbishments involving structural changes or system upgrades may span several months. We always provide detailed schedules upfront to minimise disruption to your business.
How can disruption be minimised during an office refurbishment? ▼
We offer phased delivery, out-of-hours working and weekend working to keep your business running throughout the office refurbishment. Our project managers plan works carefully to isolate active zones and provide regular updates so you're never caught off guard.
What are the costs of office refurbishment services? ▼
Office refurbishment costs depend on the scope, size and specification of your project. We provide transparent, itemised quotes after a free no-obligation site visit — no hidden fees, no surprises. Call us on 020 3393 9600 or use the form below to get started.
Do you provide office interior design services? ▼
We do not provide interior design services. We work from your brief, existing plans, or alongside your chosen designer. Our expertise is in the expert delivery and project management of the physical office refurbishment works.
Which areas do you cover for office refurbishment? ▼
Our primary coverage area for office refurbishment projects is London, Kent, Surrey, Essex and the wider South East. We also take on projects nationally for established clients. Get in touch to discuss your location.
